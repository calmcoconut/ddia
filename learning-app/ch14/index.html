Interleaved Scenario Challenge

Mixes all topics · Builds discrimination · Forces transfer · ~10 min

Each scenario requires you to combine knowledge from multiple sections of Chapter 14. This deliberate mixing forces your brain to select the right framework — the hallmark of expert-level understanding.

Scenario 1 of 4

🌆 Smart City Traffic & Surveillance

A municipality installs a network of cameras and sensors to optimize traffic flow. Later, the police department requests real-time access to track suspects, and a marketing firm offers to buy foot-traffic data to display targeted ads on digital billboards.

Scenario 2 of 4

🛵 Gig Economy Algorithmic Management

A food delivery platform uses an algorithm to assign deliveries, calculate pay, and evaluate courier performance. Couriers who reject too many low-paying orders or show slower delivery speeds have their accounts automatically suspended.

Scenario 3 of 4

🏫 EdTech Predictive Student Grading

A school district implements a machine learning system to predict which students are at risk of failing and to recommend personalized learning resources. The model uses students' socioeconomic status, family history of graduation, and past grades.

Scenario 4 of 4

🔀 Trade-Off Tribunal

You are the Chief Ethics and Technology Officer at a global tech conglomerate. You must rule on a major architectural trade-off that pits commercial interests against user rights.
