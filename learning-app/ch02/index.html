Interleaved Scenario Challenge

Mixes all topics · Builds discrimination · Forces transfer · ~10 min

Each scenario requires you to combine knowledge from multiple sections of Chapter 2. This deliberate mixing forces your brain to select the right framework — the hallmark of expert-level understanding.

Scenario 1 of 4

🎟  High-Demand Ticket Sale Platform

You are designing the backend for a concert ticketing system:

  • Normal traffic is 50 requests per second.
  • During a major celebrity ticket sale, traffic spikes to 150,000 requests per second in a single minute.
  • The system must ensure that no seat is sold twice (double-booked).
  • Users expect immediate confirmation of their ticket reservation.
Scenario 2 of 4

🚚  IoT Logistics Telemetry System

You run a telemetry system for a fleet of 500,000 delivery trucks:

  • Each truck sends its GPS location, speed, and engine temperature every 10 seconds (50,000 writes per second).
  • Logistics operators view real-time fleet maps showing truck locations.
  • A maintenance team runs weekly analytical queries to find trucks with engine wear (scanning historical data).
Scenario 3 of 4

🏦  Financial Ledger & Audit Log

You are building the core ledger for a digital bank:

  • Reliability is the absolute highest priority: no transaction can ever be lost or corrupted.
  • Security auditors require an immutable log of every change.
  • The business grows from 10,000 users to 10 million users over two years.
Scenario 4 of 4

🔀  Trade-Off Tribunal

You must make a ruling on each trade-off. There is no "right answer" — only well-reasoned arguments.
