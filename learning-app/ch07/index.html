Each scenario requires you to combine knowledge from multiple sections of Chapter 7. This deliberate mixing forces your brain to select the right framework — the hallmark of expert-level understanding.

Scenario 1 of 4

📈 Financial High-Frequency Trading Log System

A financial firm needs a system to store trading execution logs:

  • 10 million transactions per day from worldwide markets.
  • Traders need to query their own executions in real-time (reads by trader ID).
  • Risk compliance needs to run end-of-day auditing reports scanning the entire day's volume.
  • The system must handle high write spikes during market opening/closing hours.
Scenario 2 of 4

🛍️ Global E-Commerce Platform

You are designing a database for a global shopping platform:

  • 50 million active shoppers worldwide.
  • Shoppers search for products by category (e.g. "Electronics", "Clothing") and filter by price.
  • Fast checkout writes are critical.
  • European customer data must comply with GDPR storage residency requirements.
Scenario 3 of 4

🎮 Multiplayer Online Game (MMO)

A gaming company is launching an MMO with:

  • 10 million players.
  • Players are grouped into 'guilds' (teams of up to 100 players).
  • Game state updates (health, coordinates) are written every 100ms.
  • A leaderboard shows the top guilds in real-time.
