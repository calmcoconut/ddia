Interleaved Scenario Challenge

Mixes all topics · Builds discrimination · Forces transfer · ~10 min

Each scenario requires you to combine knowledge from multiple sections of Chapter 3. This deliberate mixing forces your brain to select the right framework — the hallmark of expert-level understanding.

Scenario 1 of 4

🏥 Healthcare Patient Portal & Gene Graph

You are building a healthcare portal that handles:

  • Patient Profiles: Personal information, basic medical records, allergies (normally read together).
  • Genetic Research Tool: A highly connected graph tracking gene mutations, symptoms, and relationships (mutations trigger symptoms, symptoms share indicators).
  • Daily Nurse Logs: High-velocity logs of nurse check-ins (temperature, blood pressure, timestamp).
Scenario 2 of 4

✈️ Flight Booking & Baggage Log

You are the lead architect for a global airline reservation system. The system must support:

  • Search & Booking: Customers searching for flights, selecting seats, and booking tickets (high concurrency, transactional).
  • Baggage Tracking: A real-time log of every scan of a passenger's bag (checked in, loaded on flight, unloaded, at carousel).
  • Route Optimization: Analyzing passenger booking patterns across the globe to optimize flight paths and pricing (OLAP).
Scenario 3 of 4

🎵 Music Streaming Service

You are designing the data infrastructure for a music streaming service:

  • User Catalog: Playlists created by users containing lists of tracks.
  • Music Social Net: Users following artists, following friends, and sharing playlists.
  • Machine Learning Recommendations: A data science team needs to build a collaborative filtering recommendation model using user listen history.
Scenario 4 of 4

🔀 Trade-Off Tribunal

You must make a ruling on each trade-off. There is no "right answer" — only well-reasoned arguments.
